Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

At Gamedevunit, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Dutch privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

We operate a static website designed to showcase our capabilities. We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when using the contact form on our website:

  • Name: To address you properly in our correspondence.
  • Email Address: To communicate with you and reply to your inquiry.
  • Studio/Organization: To understand the company you represent.
  • Interest & Needs: To assess your game development requirements and prepare for our engagement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected via the contact form solely for business purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries, questions, or requests for proposal.
  • To assess potential collaboration or contract opportunities.
  • To maintain communication during a business engagement.

We do not use your information for marketing newsletters, automated profiling, or any unrelated commercial purposes. We never sell, lease, or distribute your personal details to third parties.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, our legal bases for processing your personal data are:

  • Consent: By filling out and submitting our contact form, you explicitly consent to us processing the data to reply to your request.
  • Pre-contractual Measures: When your inquiry relates to hiring us or exploring a co-development partnership, processing is necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website is built using static technologies and does not use tracking cookies, analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixel), or any other user-tracking mechanisms. Your browsing activity on this website is completely private.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including satisfying any business, legal, or reporting requirements. If our communication does not lead to a business relationship, your contact data will be securely deleted.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your personal data (the "right to be forgotten").
  • The right to withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Gamedevunit@gmail.com.
