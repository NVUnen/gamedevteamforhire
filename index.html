Operational History // 20+ Years

Over the past 20 years, we have worked on some of the world's best known videogame franchises. We developed two separate mobile entries in the Halo franchise for Microsoft, contributed to Assassin's Creed Nexus as a co-dev party and delivered a full blown installment in the Metro franchise. In the form of Metro Awakening, next to 15+ other games. We have worked on mobile, VR console and PC and everything in between and always to the satisfaction of our partners, delivering on time, to budget and to quality!
